The Problem

Poor requirements cost programmes millions.

Studies consistently show that 40-50% of defects originate in requirements. Vague language, passive voice, untestable statements, and missing traceability propagate through design, build, and test — compounding cost at every stage. Most teams catch these issues too late, or not at all.
